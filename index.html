My name is Darrin Scott Hunter (the Dish in Dish Design), and I'm a designer. I create experiences across spatial, digital, and printed media by shamelessly appropriating methods of fine art to lower the conscious threshold of aesthetic awareness. Once visitors, users, and readers become aware that a design solution transcends the mundane, even in small ways, surprise and delight suddenly become possible. They are there—just inside a doorway, underneath a mouse rollover, sliding off a page edge—waiting to be discovered, an awakening, a new path, a destination yet unknown.
